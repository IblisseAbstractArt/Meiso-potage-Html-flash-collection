■ About the Work

This is a game where Sunny keeps running away from the hunters,
kind of like that certain TV show.

You just move Sunny and keep escaping while dodging the eyes of the black lilies.
It's a really simple game.
When in a pinch, use "run" and "hide" skillfully to escape.

"Run" makes you run at twice the normal speed.
However, if your stamina runs out, you won't be able to run, so watch the "yellow gauge" at the top left.

"Hide" makes Sunny invisible.
In this state, you won't be found by enemies,
but if you hide after you've already been spotted, they will keep chasing you.
In that case, it's good to use corners to hide where they can't see you.
This also consumes the "green gauge" at the top left, so be careful.

Both gauges gradually recover over time.


In the forest, there are [P] items and mushrooms lying around,
so it's good to pick them up.
[P] extends the green "hide" gauge, and mushrooms extend the yellow "run" gauge.
Since the number of enemies increases in the latter half, it’s advantageous to collect items early.
(Of course, the more you move around, the higher the risk of being spotted by enemies...)


...Well, that's about the content and tips.

Lunachild... since the system doesn't have elements like footsteps, it's hard to express her ability...


■ Controls
Arrow keys: Move. Double-tap to dash. (Consumes stamina)
Shift key: Hide. You won't be found while in this state (except if already spotted)
