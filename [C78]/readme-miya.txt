=====================================================
　　"Cirno's Ramune Burst Three-Stage Rocket"
=====================================================



【STORY】

Cirno and the others stole a rare bottle of Ramune from Eientei.
However, it was not Ramune, but a medicine made by Eirin
that "makes even the biggest idiot smart"!

Without knowing this, Cirno drank it,
and suddenly demonstrated incredible calculation ability, drawing a certain conclusion...

───With these three bottles of Ramune, if I repeatedly blast in midair,
I should be able to break through Earth's gravity and fly far beyond the sky!
I'm such a genius!


【Greetings】

Hello~
This is Mi~ya, the nominal representative of Meisou Potage.
If this is your first time, nice to meet you; if not, thank you for your continued support.

Thank you very much for picking up Meisou Potage's "Ramune Flavor".

This is part of the handmade CD "Flavor" series,
and this time, since it's Ramune flavor,
I tried making a game using Ramune.

...That said, I was thinking of making something else until just before,
but things looked uncertain, so I suddenly came up with this game.
When people are pressed, all sorts of wisdom comes out...

Despite the sudden request,
I am truly grateful to Detchi-san for allowing me to use the music...

Now then, as my work in the "Flavor" series, is this already a tradition?
This time too, it's a "super inflation, one-shot clear" style game.
...Well, in this case, maybe "three shots"?

I was thinking about featuring Ramune and Cirno... and various things,
and suddenly I pictured Cirno saying "Salad bar" and falling,
and before I knew it, this game was created.
The way the character is handled is worse than ever.
But I put more love into it than usual.


The "Three-Stage Rocket" is a homage to Kanjuden.
The operation steps do not change for all three times,
but from the second stage onward, a time limit appears,
adding a chicken race element that I think creates a nice tension.

By the way, the time limit varies depending on the power of the previous Ramune.
If you blast off vigorously, you can earn more time to shake the next Ramune.

Only for the third stage, it's the usual inflation style.
The parameters when shaking the bottle and when pushing up the cap
will greatly affect the outcome.



Anyway, I hope you enjoy it.


Finally,
to the original author ZUN,
to Detchi-san who provided the music,
and to everyone who cooperated,
thank you very much.

Well then.
