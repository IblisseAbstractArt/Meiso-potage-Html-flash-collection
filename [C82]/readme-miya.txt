■ About the Work

I started making this work
when I received a CD from katsu of DiGiTAL WiNG at last year's Winter Comiket.
(Thank you very much, katsu!)

I was deeply moved by the song "3x3x3m Girl" included on the CD,
and based on this song, I decided to try making a silly and slightly sexy PV animation.

Because the music is fun and the lyrics are a bit naughty,
I was able to work on it enthusiastically and enjoyably during production.

In the end, I hope to include various characters and pairings,
to create a work that will make you grin.
(...within the range suitable for all ages)


This time, as it is a trailer, it is a short piece,
but this winter (C83), together with Tetora-san,
we plan to release the completed work,
so please look forward to it.

You can only hear a little of the song in the trailer, but it is a very enjoyable tune,
so if you are not familiar with it, why not take this opportunity to pick up a CD from DiGiTAL WiNG
and enjoy the full version?          Stealth marketing, huh? ＞(ﾟДﾟ )
