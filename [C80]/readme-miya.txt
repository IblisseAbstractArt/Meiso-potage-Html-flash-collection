Hello, this is Mi~ya.
Thank you very much for picking up Meisou Potage "Cola Flavor."

The now customary Meisou Potage Touhou handmade CD "○○ Flavor" series,
mine, as usual, is a score-inflation type action game.
That said, it is not the usual "one-shot clear" type game,
but rather a fairly normal action game where you earn more time and keep playing.

Also, this time, because the gameplay involves swinging up, down, left, and right like a pendulum,
I feel it might be a bit easier to get motion sickness compared to other 2D games.
I myself felt sick after continuous test playing during development... orz

Therefore, it is better to avoid playing for long periods as much as possible.
If you start feeling unwell, please stop playing without pushing yourself and rest your eyes and brain.

I hope you enjoy playing the game even a little.

■ Game content

[Overview]
"Extend a thread toward the ceiling, swing forward like a pendulum, then release the thread and launch forward."
Basically, this is repeated.
While advancing, avoid Kisume’s interference and collect items.
Aim to earn as high a score as possible.

[Items]
・Time extension item:
　Collecting this slightly extends the time limit.
・Score item:
　Collecting this adds to your score.
　The score added increases significantly as your combo count rises.

* The combo count increases if you collect the next item within a certain time after collecting an item.

[Enemy]
・Kisume:
　If you hit her, your life decreases and you lose speed.

[Game over conditions]
・Life reaches 0
・Time limit reaches 0
・Failing to release the thread and falling down

■ Controls
[Mouse input]
Hold down mouse button:
　Yamame extends the thread in that direction.
Release mouse button:
　Yamame lets go of the thread.

[Key input (while swinging)]
Up key:
　Shorten the thread
Down key:
　Lengthen the thread
Left/Right keys:
　Adjust the swinging momentum like a swing
