　　　　　■■■ Mokotan Has Logged In!? 3 ■■■

Hello there. This is Kagi from Meisou Potage.
The height of summer is here and the hot and humid days continue, how is your health?

Now then, this time we have the third installment of the now classic crappy game series
"Mokotan Has Logged In!?"
You ask where the second installment is? Well, it was included in another project a while back...
Or rather, shouldn't we stop recycling materials already?

The rules this time are: Mokotan appears randomly from one of nine holes, and you have to
"tap repeatedly" exactly as many times as the number of lines of dialogue. Be careful not to tap too many times or hit a spot where Mokotan isn’t, or your score will be deducted.

Depending on how many Mokotans you defeat, the points you earn for defeating the next Mokotan will also increase.
Therefore, the fewer lines of dialogue there are, the more points you can earn,
but that pattern is completely random. So real luck is also required.
How is that as a game...?

Please feel free to send your impressions or complaints after playing via email, messenger, Twitter, or Google+.

That’s about it.
