<About the Game>

This time, with the concept of "Fairy Wars,"
I tried making a simple falling block puzzle game.

The thrill of freezing a bunch of bullet patterns all at once and clearing them in one go
is something that also applies to puzzle games,
so I wanted to recreate that feeling...

I'm sorry it turned out half-baked!
There were parts like effects and such that I wanted to put more effort into,
but in the end, I couldn't fix the bugs and had to reluctantly leave them unimplemented.
Ah, but there might still be some bugs left...

In the future, I'd like to try porting this to iPhone or Android.


<About the BGM>

The original song is "Tomboyish Love Girl."

What a disaster.
