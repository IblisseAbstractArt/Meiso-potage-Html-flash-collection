■■■ Mini Game: Reimu's Precious Amulet ■■■

Hello there. This is Kagi from Meisou Potage.
The Reitaisai has finally arrived. At an unprecedentedly large-scale only event,
we have brought an unprecedentedly crappy game.

This time, we've had Reimu take the starring role after a long time.
It's a "comp gacha" crappy game where you try to collect the precious amulets
while minimizing the amount of money invested.

Basically, it's a luck-based game where you just keep drawing cards.
Almost all the card illustrations are taken from my past works.
(The absence of the Shinreibyou characters is intentional. Sorry, everyone.)
In a way, this is a valuable work where you can trace my own history.

Reimu's reactions change depending on how much money you invest,
so try multiple times and see all the patterns!

Feel free to send me your impressions or complaints via email or Twitter.

By the way, at this Reitaisai, I am also distributing a collection of video works
under the circle "KGYK" that I established (or at least I should be).
Since you are reading this, the event is probably already over,
but please check that out as well.

That's about it.
Thank you for your continued support.
