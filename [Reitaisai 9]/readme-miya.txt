[Greetings]

Hello!
Thank you very much for picking up Meisou Potage "Red and White Manju Flavor."

This time for the Reitaisai, since ZUN, also known as the Shrine Maiden, is getting married,
we wanted something celebratory, so we went with "Red and White Manju."
(And since it's "Red and White" "Manju," the jacket features Yukkuri Reimu)


Now then, this mini-game features Patchy-san.
It's a title (logo) joke that I've "had the title in mind for a while,"
but the game content is more polished than ever,
and I think it turned out to be a fairly challenging puzzle game.

Where did the usual inflation silly game series go...?


At first, I was thinking of a puzzle that links about five types of "Yukkuri" in a coupling style
to match the CD title of "Red and White Manju" (?), 
but while thinking about it, I remembered this title joke about Patchy-san,
and I feel the system ended up having a pretty good affinity with the joke.


Unlike other puzzle games,
the main mechanic is not "matching the same color" but "matching different colors,"
so you might be quite confused while playing.
I tried to make the interface as frustration-free as possible regarding that,
so at first, please just rely on your intuition and play casually, and I would be glad.
