Hello. This is Mi~ya.

Thank you very much for picking up the wandering potage "Curry Flavor."

So, this time it's a Youmu mini-game.

Slash every falling leaf without leaving a single one...
I think everyone has at least once associated this with the "Sword Master."

In this game, when you drag the mouse on the screen, Youmu swings her sword,
cutting the leaves along the drag path in half.
The path itself can be fairly rough,
but if the drag is too slow or too perpendicular to the leaf's dotted line, you'll miss.

As time passes, the difficulty increases, and your score rises accordingly.
Also, if you slash precisely along the dotted line, slash continuously without missing, or cut two or more leaves at the same time,
the points you earn will multiply even more.

It's quite a simple game,
but I hope you enjoy it.
