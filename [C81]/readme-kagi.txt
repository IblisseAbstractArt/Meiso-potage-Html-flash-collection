　　　　　■■■ Mokotan Logged In!? 4 ■■■

Hello there. This is Kagi from Meisou Potage.
Time flies, and it’s already the end of 2011.

This time, it’s the fourth installment of the now classic crappy game series “Mokotan Logged In!?”
How long are you going to drag this joke out...?
It can’t be helped. I got the idea from Shinreibyou.

Basically, it’s the usual whack-a-mole, but this time, besides Mokotan,
some amusing friends will occasionally log in too!

■ Mokotan
A human who doesn’t die. Keep hitting her.

■ Pokotan
A terrifying youkai.
Show no mercy to those who harm humans! Hitting her scores triple points!

■ Mikotan
An old respected human.
Hitting her is sacrilegious! Your score will be deducted.

Quickly hit Mokotan and Pokotan while skillfully avoiding Mikotan
to aim for a high score.
If you hit Mikotan quickly, your score will be greatly reduced. Be careful!

Feel free to send your impressions or complaints via email, messenger, Twitter,
Google+, or other means.

That’s about it.
Thank you for your continued support.
